Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to convert images to PDF?

Conversion is instant! Most images are converted to PDF within a few seconds, depending on the number and size of images.

Is my data secure?

Yes! All processing happens locally on your device. Your images are never uploaded to any server, ensuring complete privacy and security.

What image formats are supported?

We support JPG, PNG, GIF, BMP, TIFF, and WebP formats. You can convert multiple images at once.

Is there a file size limit?

There's no hard limit, but we recommend keeping individual image files under 10MB for optimal performance.

Do I need to create an account?

No! Our service is completely free and doesn't require registration or login.

How can I report a bug?

You can report bugs by creating an issue on our GitHub repository or emailing us at shakirmhussain12@gmail.com.

Can I contribute to the project?

Absolutely! We welcome contributions. Check out our GitHub repository to get started with development.

Is there a mobile app?

Our web application works seamlessly on mobile devices and tablets. We're exploring mobile app development for the future.
