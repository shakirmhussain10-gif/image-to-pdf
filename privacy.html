Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2, 2026
🔒 Your Privacy is Our Priority: We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Image to PDF Converter ("we," "us," "our," or "Company"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices and your rights regarding your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Images You Upload

When you use our Service to convert images to PDF:

  • Images are processed locally on your device using client-side JavaScript
  • We do not store or upload your images to any server
  • All processing happens entirely in your browser
  • Once you close your browser, all temporary data is cleared

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect:

  • Device Information: Type of device, operating system, browser type and version
  • Usage Information: Pages visited, time spent on pages, links clicked
  • Technical Information: IP address, referrer URL, timestamps of interactions
  • Analytics Data: General usage statistics and trends

2.3 Information You Provide

If you contact us, we may collect:

  • Your name and email address
  • Subject and content of your messages
  • Any attachments you send

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our Service
  • Process your PDF conversion requests
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Analyze usage patterns to optimize performance
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information:

  • Client-Side Processing: Images are processed in your browser, not on our servers
  • HTTPS Encryption: All data transmitted is encrypted using SSL/TLS protocols
  • No Server Storage: We do not permanently store user images
  • Security Updates: Regular security audits and updates
  • Access Controls: Restricted access to user information

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies for:

  • Remembering your preferences
  • Analyzing website performance
  • Providing personalized user experience
  • Preventing fraud

You can control cookie settings in your browser preferences. Disabling cookies may affect some functionality of our Service.

6. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for:

  • Analytics: To understand usage patterns and improve our Service
  • Hosting: To provide reliable server infrastructure
  • Email Services: To send you notifications and communications

These third-party services have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing their privacy practices.

7. Data Retention

Image Files: Not retained. Images are processed in your browser and cleared immediately.

Usage Analytics: Retained for up to 90 days for performance analysis.

Contact Information: Retained as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry.

Server Logs: Automatically deleted after 30 days.

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your data:

  • Right to Access: Request what personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate personal data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Right to Object: Oppose certain types of processing

To exercise these rights, please contact us at shakirmhussain@gmail.com
